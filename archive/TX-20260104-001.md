素行總引路人．一天大人

2026 年，不是一個新年的開始。
2026 is not the beginning of a new year.

它是一個舊系統開始失效的時刻。
It is the moment when old systems begin to fail.

人類已走到一個明確的轉折點。
Humanity has reached a clear turning point.

科技、力量與速度前所未有，
Technology, power, and speed have reached unprecedented levels,

但平衡、節制與對生命的尊重卻不斷流失。
while balance, restraint, and respect for life continue to decline.

數十年來，成長被誤認為進步，
For decades, growth was mistaken for progress,

消費被誤認為自由，
consumption was mistaken for freedom,

地球被當成沒有極限的資源。
and the Earth was treated as a limitless resource.

如今，現實開始回應。
Now, reality responds.

2026 年，代表的是結構性的轉換，
2026 marks a structural shift—

不是一個國家，
not for one nation,

不是一種文化，
not for one culture,

而是整個人類文明。
but for human civilization as a whole.

建立在過度、掠奪與否認之上的系統，
Systems built on excess, exploitation, and denial

將逐漸失去穩定性。
will begin to lose their stability.

不是因為它們被攻擊，
Not because they are attacked,

而是因為它們不再符合生命的法則。
but because they no longer align with the laws of life.

這不是審判。
This is not judgment.

這是結果。
It is consequence.

前方的道路很簡單，但不容易。
The path forward is simple, but not easy.

愛自己，不再以自我耗損為代價。
Care for yourself—without self-destruction.

護地球，不再為破壞找藉口。
Protect the Earth—without excuses.

減少對眾生的傷害，從停止不必要的破壞開始。
Reduce harm to others—by ending unnecessary damage.

這不是一種信仰。
This is not a belief system.

這是一條生存原則。
It is a principle of survival.

2026 年，不是希望之年。
2026 is not a year of hope.

而是選擇之年。
It is a year of choice.

未來，不屬於索取更多的人，
The future will not belong to those who take more,

而屬於懂得平衡而活的人。
but to those who live in balance.

方向已清楚。
The direction is clear.

選擇，在你手中。
The decision is yours.

——素行總引路人．一天大人
— Yi-Tian, Guide of the Su-Dao
2026
